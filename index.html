About Us

Kongu Blood Bank, Erode, is committed to serving the community by ensuring the availability of safe blood through voluntary donations. We maintain the highest standards of quality and safety in all our operations.

Mission

To provide a reliable supply of safe blood products to all patients in need, while promoting voluntary blood donation as a life-saving service and maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.

Vision

To become the leading blood bank in the region, recognized for excellence in blood services, community engagement, and innovative approaches to meeting transfusion needs.
